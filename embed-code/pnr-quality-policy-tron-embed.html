PNR Quality Policy

Safe, Comfortable, and Economical Rail Service for Public Good

As a pioneer in Philippine railway transport, PNR is committed to dependable operations that keep passenger and freight movement reliable, secure, and service-centered.

TRON Priorities

  • Train and track serviceability to protect continuity and safety of operations.
  • Results-focused mobility support for nation building and broader public benefit.
  • Optimal delivery standards that improve customer satisfaction.
  • Necessary legal, statutory, and regulatory compliance in every service area.
  • Ongoing enhancement of the Quality Management System through continuous improvement.

PNR continues to move people and goods through competent, courteous, accountable, and service-oriented public servants.
